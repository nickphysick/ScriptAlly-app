One title, five hero-band colourways

Option 1's structure throughout — a single "Packages & materials" title up top, hero band below — with the manuscript selector tucked into the top of the materials rail (the rail is scoped to that manuscript, so it belongs there). Only the band's colourway changes. The four step icons keep their semantic tints (letter / package / plane / chart), so they read consistently against any band.

The original — soft rosy band with a faint sheen, deep-burgundy heading. Warm and inviting; ties to your To-do band so it feels native.

How they sit

Pink (1) is the warmest and most native to your existing system. Sage (2) is the most premium/editorial and harmonises with your dashboard's sage headers — my quiet favourite for a Pro feature. Burgundy (3) is the showstopper if you want this page to feel like a flagship. Honey (4) best matches the worktable's tactile mood. Cream tricolour (5) is the grown-up restraint play. If you want it unmistakably "premium calm" I'd take 2; if you want "warm and characterful" to match the desk, 1 or 4.
