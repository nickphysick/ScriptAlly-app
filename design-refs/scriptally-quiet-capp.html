Quiet Cappuccino — type coding as accent, not surface

The rule, borrowed from the dashboard's single-hue decision: in Cappuccino, type tints leave the chrome (tiles, pills, slot headers, band headers all go foam/white with mocha or burgundy ink) and survive only as whisper accents — a thin tint bar or 7px dot beside the glyph.
